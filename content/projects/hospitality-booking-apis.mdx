## Overview

Developed and maintained production RESTful APIs powering room booking, table reservations, and food ordering for global hospitality brands including The Leela, Club Marriott, and Radisson — deployed across 5 countries.

## Integrations

- **Razorpay** payment gateway with PCI-compliant security controls
- **Google Wallet** for digital pass generation and automated expiration workflows
- **Salesforce CRM** to PostgreSQL data-sync pipeline eliminating ~8 hours/week of manual reconciliation
- **Mule** middleware for enterprise system connectivity

## Key Results

- 50K+ monthly active users across hospitality properties
- Rs.5Cr+ in monthly transaction volume with 99.9% uptime
- ~25% reduction in manual hospitality check-in time via Google Wallet passes

## Scale & Reliability

Designed APIs with idempotent payment flows, retry logic for third-party integrations, and comprehensive logging for operations teams managing multi-property deployments.
